Abdulmajeed Al Sheraim is one of Saudi Arabia's foremost voices in strategic procurement, supply chain transformation, and contract management.

Over two decades, he has risen through the ranks of procurement — from early roles at Saudi Aramco to becoming the Contracts, Procurement, and Supply Chain Portfolio Director at Strategic Sourcing Sphere, where he leads enterprise sourcing strategies driving millions in value. His expertise spans the full procurement lifecycle: vendor selection, contract negotiation, risk management, supplier governance, ESG integration, and digital transformation. He is a practitioner who thinks like a strategist, and a leader who mentors like a teacher.

As a Portfolio Director at Strategic Sourcing Sphere, Abdulmajeed helps organizations unlock value from their procurement and contracting functions, with a proven track record in major infrastructure projects across Saudi Arabia. His work earned national recognition for enhancing sustainable contracting practices aligned with Saudi Vision 2030 goals in ESG, supplier diversity, and economic localization.

Abdulmajeed is also a published author — co-creator of the first-of-its-kind Arabic Contracts, Procurement, and Supply Chain Dictionary, published by King Salman Global Academy for Arabic Language. This landmark publication sets a new standard for professional terminology in Arabic-speaking markets globally.
